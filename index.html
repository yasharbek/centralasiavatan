Our Story

Central Asia Vatan was first founded in 2000 in Queens, New York under the name of "Vatan Asia". As a not-for-profit organization, our primary goal has always been fostering community among members of the Central Asian diaspora in the United States. Throughout the past decade, we've organized city-wide cultural events, held numerous community gatherings, celebrated Central Asian and local holidays, and provided support for the members of our diaspora through volunteer work and community initiatives.

Our Mission

Serving a diverse population of Central Asian immigrants in the United States, Central Asia Vatan strives to:

  • organize a multi-ethnic and multi-racial coalition consisting of Central Asian immigrants residing in the United States
  • create space for the celebration of Central Asian customs, traditions, and holidays among the members of the diaspora
  • amplify the rich heritage of music, visual arts, storytelling, and other forms of expression of the Central Asian diaspora
